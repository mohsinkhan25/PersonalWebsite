Shell Eco-Marathon

Braking and Steering Team Member

During my early years of college, I volunteered my time to support the Texas A&M Shell Eco-Marathon team. This team was a group of seniors working on their mechanical engineering senior design project. Our goal was to develop a fuel efficient vehicle within certain parameters and rules set by Shell. As a volunteer, I spent time designing, manufacturing and testing a large portion of the braking, steering and suspension components of our vehicle. While I was unable to see the team compete, it was a wonderful experience.
